Quick Bio

Hawk Licari is a versatile left-handed pitcher, outfielder, and first baseman from Scottsdale, AZ, in the class of 2027. With an athletic build (6-0, 170 lbs), he features a clean, repeatable delivery from the stretch, a fastball topping 87 mph with armside run, a sharp curveball (68-74 mph) and slider (73-75 mph), and a developing changeup (70-73 mph). As a left-handed hitter, Hawk showcases quick bat speed (up to 76 mph), exit velocities reaching 100 mph, and a middle-of-the-field approach with pull-side power. He's a solid runner (60-yard dash: 7.20 sec) with outfield arm strength up to 86 mph. Attending Pinnacle High School, Hawk has excelled in showcases like the PG Jr. National and PBR Future Games, consistently improving his metrics. Aspiring to play collegiate baseball, he focuses on team success and personal growth through dedicated training.
